波场首席执行官孙宇晨向马云创立的湖畔大学捐赠 150 万美元


根据 4 月 8 日 CoinTelegraph 发布的一份新闻稿，波场首席执行官孙宇晨向阿里巴巴联合创始人马云创办的湖畔大学捐赠了 150 万美元。湖畔大学由马云于 2015 年创办，旨在帮助企业家和企业领导人学习在中国市场进一步发展和推广所需的核心业务技能。据称，孙宇晨此次的捐赠将支持下一届学生，他们是来自 14 个行业的 41 名学员，其中约一半来自互联网行业，另一半来自传统行业。孙宇晨在谈到这所大学对专业发展的重要性时说：“湖畔大学对我和公司产生了巨大的影响。没有湖畔大学，波场就不会有今天的规模。我的创业精神就是在那里得到启蒙和发展的。”


Tron CEO Justin Sun Donates $1.5 Million to Alibaba Co-Founder Jack Ma’s University


Tron and BitTorrent CEO Justin Sun has donated $1.5 million to the Hupan University established by Alibaba’s co-founder Jack Ma, according to a press release shared with Cointelegraph on April 8. The Hupan University was launched in 2015 by Ma in a bid to help entrepreneurs and business owners learn core business skills needed for further development and promotion in the Chinese market. Sun’s contribution will purportedly support the next class of 41 students from 14 industries, with about half representing the Internet sector and half the traditional sector. Commenting on the importance of the university to professional development, Sun said, “Hupan University made a huge impact and difference to me and the company. Without Hupan, Tron will not be what it is today. My entrepreneurship was enlightened and developed there.”

立陶宛财政部为加密货币公司引入法律修正案


The Baltic Times Reports 于 4 月 8 日报道，立陶宛共和国财政部计划公布在该国经营加密货币相关业务的法律修正案。根据报告，对于与加密货币交易所、加密钱包运营商以及首次代币发行（ICO）有关的公司，立陶宛财政部希望此举能为其运营带来更多的法律确定性。文章写道，通过在该行业实施法律要求，当局努力确保针对反洗钱和恐怖主义融资的有效政策，并对消费者进行保护。新的修正案提议，与加密货币相关的公司必须在注册中心注册后才能作为法人实体运营。这些公司还必须执行与反洗钱和恐怖融资相关的法律，并严格遵守了解你的客户（KYC）规则，包括向金融犯罪调查局（FCIS）报告大额的金融交易。


Lithuanian Finance Ministry to Introduce Legal Amendments for Crypto-Related Firms


The Republic of Lithuania’s Ministry of Finance plans to release legal amendments for operating crypto-related businesses in the country, The Baltic Times reports on April 8. According to the report, the Lithuanian finance ministry wants to bring more legal certainty to the operation of companies relating to cryptocurrency exchanges, crypto wallet operators, as well as initial coin offerings (ICOs). By enforcing legal requirements in the industry, the authority reportedly seeks to ensure an efficient policy against money laundering and terrorism financing, as well as to guarantee a due level of consumer protection, the articles writes. The new amendments propose that crypto-related companies will have to be registered with the Center of Registers in order to operate as legal entities. The companies will also have to execute the Law on the Prevention of Money Laundering and Terrorist Financing, as well as to strictly observe Know Your Customer (KYC) laws including reporting high volume financial transactions to the Financial Crime Investigation Service (FCIS).

通用和宝马支持自动驾驶汽车的区块链数据共享


CoinDesk 称，通用汽车和宝马正在考虑将区块链技术纳入移动开放区块链计划（MOBI）。两个汽车巨头都有意在区块链中共享自动驾驶汽车收集的数据。据宝马公司称，一些汽车制造商正在将这些数据保存起来，这就对自动驾驶汽车的采用构成了“严重障碍”。MOBI 致力于自动驾驶汽车数据市场，该市场将在制造商之间共享自动驾驶汽车数据。通用汽车在去年 12 月为这种系统申请了专利。宝马集团区块链主管 Andre Luckow 说：“随着区块链的出现，去中心化的数据管理会以一种保护隐私和高效的方式实现。此外，如去中心化机器学习、安全多方机密计算和去中心化数据市场等新兴技术将为自主时代的数据处理提供结构。”


GM, BMW Back Blockchain Data Sharing For Self-Driving Cars


General Motors and BMW are considering the use of blockchain technology as part of the Mobility Open Blockchain Initiative (MOBI), CoinDesk writes. Both auto giants are interested in sharing data gathered by autonomous vehicles on a blockchain. According to BMW, car makers are keeping the data to themselves, which is posing as a “major barrier” in the adoption of self-driving cars. MOBI is working on autonomous vehicle data markets which will share self-driving car data between the manufacturers. GM filed a patent for such a system in December. “With the advent of blockchain, decentral[ized] data management can be implemented in a privacy-preserving and efficient way,” said Andre Luckow, BMW Group’s blockchain lead. “Further, emerging technologies, such as decentral machine learning, secure multi-party confidential computing, and decentral data markets, will provide the fabric for data processing in the autonomous age.”

英国银行 Kleinwort Hambros 推出区块链交易所交易债券


总部位于伦敦的银行 Kleinwort Hambros 宣布推出交易所交易债券（ETN）。新的 ETN 将在卢森堡上市，目标是区块链业务。ETN 仅向银行客户提供，最低投资额为 1000 英镑（约 1300 美元）。ETN 是一种在股票市场上交易的无担保、非附属债务证券，类似于交易所交易基金。根据新闻，可用此投资组合的公司是那些最有可能从区块链采用和开发中受益的公司。有 20 家公司将被纳入股票组合，他们来自不同国家和不同行业（如技术、航运、石油和天然气、托管银行和工业）。Kleinwort Hambros 的投资组合经理 John Birdwood 表示：“我们已经看到客户对区块链领域的兴趣越来越浓厚，能够通过推出第一张区块链债券来满足这一需求，我们对此非常开心。”


UK bank Kleinwort Hambros launches a blockchain ETN


London-based bank Kleinwort Hambros has announced the launch of an exchange-traded note (ETN). The new ETN is listed in Luxembourg and is targeting blockchain businesses. It will be available only to the bank’s clients, with a minimum investment of £1,000 GBP (approximately $1,300). An ETN is an unsecured, unsubordinated debt security traded on a stock market, similarly to ETFs. According to the press release, the companies available in the portfolio are those most likely to benefit from blockchain adoption and development. Twenty companies will be included in the portfolio of stocks, originating from different countries and different industries, such as technology, shipping, oil and gas, custody banking and industrials. “We have seen increasing interest from clients in the area of blockchain and we are very excited to be able to cater to this demand with the launch of our first blockchain note,” said John Birdwood, Portfolio Manager at Kleinwort Hambros.
