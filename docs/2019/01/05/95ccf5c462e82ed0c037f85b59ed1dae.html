朋友生病用语
常用生病的单词
have/get a fever 发烧;发热
have/get a cold 感冒；伤风
have /get a headache 头疼
have /get a stomachache 胃疼
have /get a heartache 心脏疼
pneumonia 肺炎
cancer 癌
Toothache牙疼
cough咳嗽
sore leg腿疼
have a sore neck/have a neck ache脖子疼
例如：
I have a cold / I get a cold 我感冒了
I get a headache 我头疼
安慰用语：
1.How are you doing?
你怎么样了？

2.I’m sorry you don’t feel well,I hope you feel better
Soon.
面对面的问候

3.I hope you can recover soon.take care yourself, ok?
希望你能很快康复过来，保重好身体哦，ok?

4.Give her my best wishes and tell her I hope she’ll get well soon.
请代我向她问好,告诉她我希望她快点好起来.

5.I hope you can get well soon.
希望你能快点好起来

英语对话
A: what’s wrong with you？
你怎么了？
B:I have a bad cold
我感冒了
A:I am sorry to hear that.Do you see doctor?
很抱歉得知这个消息，你看医生了吗？
B:Yes,the doctor said it was not serious.
是的，医生说不是很严重
A:Did the doctor give you some medicine?
医生给你开药了吗？
B:Yes.He advised me to take more rest.
是的，他建议我多休息
A:Are you better now?
你现在好些了吗？
B:Much better.
好多了
A:You have to drink more water.
你应该多喝水
B:Yes ,I will.Thank you.
是的，我会的，谢谢你
A:My pleasure.
我的荣幸

询问朋友病况
dialogue
英语情景对话
A:I haven’t seen Bill lately. How is he?
A:最近都没看到比尔。他怎么样?
B:He’s still pretty sick.
B:他仍然病得很严重。
A:That’s too bad. What does he have?
A:真糟糕。他患了什么病?
B:We don’t know, but he’s going to the doctor tomorrow.
B:我们不知道，但他打算明天去看病。
A:Let me know if there’s anything I can do.
A:如果有我能帮忙的请告诉我。
B:Thanks a lot. I’ll tell him.
