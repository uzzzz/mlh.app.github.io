李启威：将添加保密交易到莱特币上


李启威是莱特币的创始人，他计划将保密协议添加到莱特币上，并计划于2019年完成此项工作。他指出，备具争议的可替代性，“是比特币和莱特币中唯一缺失的健全货币的属性。”他说，关于扩展可扩展性的相关问题已经结束，下一个战场是可替代性和隐私性。李启威进一步说，目前他“正专注于提高莱特币的可替代性，提高的方法是添加保密协议到莱特币网络上。


Charlie Lee Wants to Add Confidential Transactions to Litecoin


Charlie Lee, the creator of Litecoin, wants to add Confidential Transactions to Litecoin and he is targeting 2019 to do so. He pointed to the controversial fungibility feature as “the only property of sound money that is missing from Bitcoin and Litecoin.” He says with the scalability issue in the rear-view mirror, fungibility and privacy are the “next battleground. Lee went on to announce that he is “now focused on making Litecoin more fungible by adding Confidential Transactions.”

荷兰考虑禁止匿名比特币交易


据荷兰媒体 Nederlandsche Omroep Stichting（NOS）最近发布的一份报道称，荷兰财政部长 Pete Hoekstra 就是否为提供加密货币服务的企业制定许可证制度寻求官方建议。据 NOS 称，因为 2017 年推动加密货币价格上涨以及刺激 ICO 发展的炒作已消失，目前对投资者进行保护和监管的紧迫性减弱了。这使得政府将注意力转移到防止加密货币被用于洗钱和资助恐怖主义的事情上。新提案建议推出一个许可证制度，要求加密货币交易所和钱包提供商对交易进行监督，并报告任何看起来不正常的交易。许可证制度也将要求交易所记录客户的信息，挺在接受调查时提供客户的详细私人信息。


Holland Considers Ban on Anonymous Bitcoin Transactions


A recent report published by Dutch media outlet, Nederlandsche Omroep Stichting (NOS) revealed that Dutch finance minister Pete Hoekstra sought official advice on whether a licensing system should be introduced for cryptocurrency services. According to NOS, the urgency for investor protection and regulation have lessened as the speculation that drove cryptocurrency prices and ICOs in 2017 has evaporated. This has led the government to shift its focus to preventing cryptocurrencies from being used for money laundering and financing terrorism. The new proposal would suggest that a licensing system that requires cryptocurrency exchanges and wallet providers to oversee transactions and report any activity that appears unusual. The licensing system would also require exchanges to store customer information and provide users’ private details in the event of an investigation.

欧亚经济委员会已准备好加密报告，并考虑进行监管


欧亚经济委员会已完成一项关于加密货币的报告，该报告旨在推动加密相关领域的监管。欧洲经济共同体一体化和宏观经济部长Tatyana Valovaya 表示，欧亚经济联盟（EAEU）希望在2025年之前为加密货币领域开发出一个综合金融市场。她强调，研究加密市场的重要性随着加密货币的增长而变得更加重要。据称，该报告侧重于对加密货币进行定义，同时也呼吁制定适用于加密领域的法律框架。Valovaya 还提到，加密货币“迟早”会对宏观经济的稳定带来更大的影响。


Eurasian Economic Commission Readies Report on Crypto and Considers Regulation


The Eurasian Economic Commission has finished a report on cryptocurrencies that is designed to prompt regulation within crypto-related sectors. Tatyana Valovaya, the EEC minister for integration and macroeconomics said that the Eurasian Economic Union (EAEU) aspires to develop a consolidated financial market for cryptocurrency by 2025 and she emphasized the importance of studying crypto-markets as their growth becomes more robust. The report is said to focus on the definition of cryptocurrencies and it also calls for the development of a crypto legal framework. Valovaya also mentioned that ‘sooner or later’ cryptocurrencies could have a greater impact on macroeconomic stability.

以太坊工具 | WeTurst 推出用于部署和管理以太坊节点的工具“Blockform”


WeTrust 是一个专注于为金融产品搭建去中心化平台的项目。近期，该公司推出了一款名为“Blockform”的新工具，该工具旨在简化用户在自己的服务器上部署和管理以太坊节点的流程。Blockform 还允许开发人员在现有的云服务器提供商（例如微软 Azure 或 亚马逊 Web Services ）上轻松创建和部署主网或测试网。使得开发人员不再需要去安装、配置和管理以太坊节点的基础设施，使他们能将更多的精力放在自己的项目上。


ETH Tool | WeTrust Launches Blockform Tool for Deploying and Managing Ethereum Nodes


WeTrust, a project focused on creating a decentralized platform for financial products, recently launched a new tool named Blockform which is designed to simplify the process of deploying and managing Ethereum nodes on your own server. Blockform allows developers to easily create and deploy mainnets or testnets on existing cloud service providers such as Microsoft Azure or Amazon Web Services. This removes the need to install, configure and manage the infrastructure of ETH nodes, allowing them to focus on their projects.
