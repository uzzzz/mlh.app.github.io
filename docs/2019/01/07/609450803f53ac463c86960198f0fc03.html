普林斯顿大学将重开备受欢迎的线上比特币和加密货币课程


随着区块链和加密货币的热度再次上涨，普林斯顿大学决定再次推出免费在线课程，帮助用户了解比特币和加密货币的真实面目。该课程为期11周，将于1月6日开课，另一套课程将于1月21日开课，这些课程将向公众开放，目前已有超过9000名学生报名参加。课程主题包括：加密区块建设（“密码学原语”）、比特币共识机制、比特币挖矿、监管问题、山寨币以及加密生态系统。


Princeton University to Restart Popular Free Online Bitcoin and Crypto Course


As interest in blockchain and cryptocurrencies is beginning to grow again, Princeton University has decided to reboot its free online class helping users separate “fact from fiction” about Bitcoin and cryptocurrency. The 11-week-long class is set to begin on January 6th with another session beginning on January 21st and is open to the public with over 9,000 students already signed up. Some of the topics covered included Cryptographic building blocks (“primitives”), Bitcoin’s consensus mechanism, Bitcoin mining, Regulation issues, and Altcoins and the crypto ecosystem.

金融稳定委员会（FSB）：加密资产不会对全球金融稳定构成威胁

FSB: Crypto-assets do not threaten global financial stability


A fresh report from the Financial Stability Board (FSB) is out, predicting what effects cryptocurrencies may have on the economy. Although it notes that cryptocurrencies are continuing to ”evolve rapidly,” the report suggests the cryptocurrencies do not threaten the economy.

以太坊核心开发团队：实施一种新的基于 GPU 的PoW 算法


以太坊（ETH）核心开发人员已经达成了初步的共识，即实施一种新的工作量证明（PoW）算法，该算法可以提高基于 GPU - 而不是基于 ASIC 的网络挖矿的效率。


Ethereum Core Devs :implement a new proof-of-work (PoW) based on GPU


Ethereum (ETH) core developers have reached a tentative consensus to implement a new proof-of-work (PoW) algorithm that would increase the efficiency of GPU-based — as opposed to ASIC-based — mining on the network.

Chris Burniske：比特币和加密货币离主流社会越来越远


Chris Burniske（著名的加密货币分析师、研究人员和作者，Placeholder 风险投资公司的合伙人，也是《加密资产》一书的合著者）在推特上评论说，主流社会已经忘记了比特币。比特币和加密货币正在从投资者的脑海中消失。然而比特币也在发展着：Jemas Loop（另一位著名的比特币领袖）在推特上指出，2018年，比特币交易投入比列从10%上升到40%；一数字交易所将为其客户提供与纳斯达克上的上市公司交易的代币交易服务；洲际交易所的 Bakkt 已做好了上线准备；美国著名广播员马克斯·凯瑟（Max Keiser）预测，从2019年开始，比特币将取代美元成为世界储备货币。


Chris Burniske:Bitcoin And Crypto Gets Further Away From Mainstream Adoption


Chris Burniske（ leading cryptocurrency analyst, researcher and author, partner at Placeholder Venture Capital and co-authored the book “Cryptoassets.” ）commented on Twitter that the mainstream has forgotten about bitcoin. Bitcoin and crypto is disappearing from investors minds.While development is happening：Jemas Loop（another prominent bitcoin leader)Tweeted that the percentage of bitcoin transactions spending segregated witness inputs rose from 10 per cent to 40 per cent during 2018; A digital exchange will offer its clients to trade with Nasdaq-listed companies in the shape of tokens; ICE’s Bakkt is about to launch and are getting ready; The well-known American broadcaster Max Keiser has now started 2019 by predicting Bitcoin will dethrone the US dollar as the world’s reserve currency.

加密货币友好派政治家 Ueli Maurer 接任瑞士总统


乌利·毛雷尔(Ueli Maurer)在过去3年里担任瑞士（最具经济影响力国家之一）的财政部长，已成为瑞士联邦主席。鉴于他的过往经历和对金融科技监管的自由主义观点，毛雷尔的当选被认为能推动这个阿尔卑斯国家加密货币产业的积极发展。


Crypto-Friendly Statesman Takes Over Swiss Presidency


After serving as finance minister of one of the most economically influential nations over the past three years, Ueli Maurer is now starting his term as President of the Swiss Confederation. Given his track record and liberal views regarding fintech regulation, Maurer’s election is considered a positive development for the Alpine nation’s expanding crypto industry.
