以太坊经典疑似遭受51%攻击


昨夜，以太坊经典官方在推特上指出，以太坊经典可能正在遭受区块链重组及双花攻击。他们随后呼吁加密交易所和 ETC 矿池在处理资金提取及存入时设置多达400次的确认次数。今天早上7点，以太坊经典在官方推特上对此事件作出澄清。推文称，此事件是因为一家加密挖矿制造商在测试一批新设备，因而进行是自私挖矿。


Ethereum Classic Might Have Been Hit by a 51% Attack


Late last night on Twitter, the official Ethereum Classic team noted that possible chain reorganization and double spend attacks are ongoing. They subsequently asked crypto exchanges and ETC mining pools to require as many as 400 confirmations for withdrawals and deposits. At 7 this morning, the Ethereum Classic Twitter account posted an update that may provide clarity on the situation. The post claims that a crypto mining manufacturer was testing out a batch of new devices and engaged in selfish mining.

Parity Technologies 获得以太坊基金会 500 万美元赞助


Parity Technologies 发布 Medium 文章宣布该公司已获得以太坊基金会 500万 美元的赞助，用于支持他们在 Casper、分片技术、轻客户端、开发者工具、QA、审计和以太坊基础设施改进等方面的工作。Parity Technologies 是一家从事开发面向开发者的免费开源工具的初创公司，旨在帮助开发者与以太坊网络进行交互以及帮助他们推出自己的 EVA 区块链。据 Parity Technologies 称，他们将通过完成 eWasm 兼容性的研发、为主网创造一个轻钱包以及完成分片阶段0和阶段1的研发工作，使得以太坊变得更完美。


Parity Technologies awarded $5 million grant by the Ethereum Foundation


Parity Technologies announced on medium that they have won $5M grant from Ethereum Foundation for their work on Casper, sharding, light clients, developer tools, QA, audits and infrastructure improvements. Parity Technologies is a startup who developes free open-source tools for developers to interact with the Ethereum network and launch their own EVM-based blockchains. According to Parity Technologies, they will now focus on making Ethereum even better by completing eWasm compatibility, shipping a light wallet for mainnet, and completing otheir work on sharding Phase 0 and 1.
