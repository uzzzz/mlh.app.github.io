Ethereum 开发者提议在2月底激活君士坦丁堡硬分叉


据开发者 Peter Szilagyi 称，君士坦丁堡硬分叉计划在 2 月 27 日区块高度达到7,280,000 的时候进行升级。以太坊核心开发人员曾提议在 2 月底激活君士坦丁堡。这一提议是在上周五的一次电话会议上提出的，参与电话会议的有以太坊创始人 Vitalik Buterin 及其他开发者，包括 Hudson Jameson、Lane Rettig、Afri Schoedon、Péter Szilágyi、Martin Holste Swende、Danny Ryan、Alexey Akhunov 等。


Ethereum Devs Propose Activating Constantinople Hard Fork in Late February


Constantinople hard fork activation has been set for block number 7,280,000, scheduled to hit on February 27th, according to developer Péter Szilágyi. Ethereum core developers have proposed activating Constantinople in late February. The proposal was made during a core developer phone call on Friday morning, and participants on the call included ethereum creator Vitalik Buterin and other developers, including Hudson Jameson, Lane Rettig, Afri Schoedon, Péter Szilágyi, Martin Holste Swende, Danny Ryan and Alexey Akhunov, among others.

报告:暗网市场的比特币使用量在2018年翻了一番


一项研究显示，暗网市场(DNMs) 的比特币使用量在2018年翻了一番，平均每天达到200万美元。比特币因其相对匿名性，长期以来被用于在暗网(darknet)上购买商品。尽管市面上有很多以隐私为中心的加密货币，但比特币似乎仍是 DNMs 上最受欢迎的加密币。


Report: Bitcoin Use on Darknet Markets Doubled in 2018


Bitcoin use on darknet markets (DNMs) doubled in 2018, a study has shown, rising to an average of $2 million per day. Bitcoin has long been used to buy goods on the darknet due to its relative anonymity, and looks set to remain a favorite on DNMs, despite the existence of more privacy-focused cryptocurrencies.
