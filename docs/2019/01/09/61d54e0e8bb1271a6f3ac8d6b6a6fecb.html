Craig S Wright

澳本聪


51%攻击是不会发生的。


为什么……


1.法律-矿工如果这样做很容易就被认定为从事犯罪行为，且会被控告侵权和合同违约。


矿工的投资巨大，投入的规模达亿万美元甚至更大。


2.经济激励-矿工如果这样做，会降低他们从激励机制得来的利润，他们从事的这种这激励机制可以提供长期的利益。


比特币并不是加密保护技术-它甚至都没有加密。


它是一个经济激励系统。


矿工不会对比特币进行51%攻击，因为这对他们来说没什么经济性的好处。

这也是为什么只有比特币能存在（只要一条区块链），并且是唯一的协议。


There is NO 51% attack.


Why...


1. Law - a miner doing this is an easy target for criminal action and can easily be taken to task in both Tort and contract.


Miners have a HUGE investment. 100s of millions of USD at scale or MORE


2. Economic incentives - if the miner does this, they lower the profitability of the very investment they have a long term interest in building.


Bitcoin is NOT at all about cryptographic protections - it is NOT even encrypted.


It is an economic incentive system.


Miners do not do 51% attacks on Bitcoin as it is not economically viable for them to.


This is also why ONLY one Bitcoin can exist (only one Blockchain) and only ever one protocol.

Emin Gün Sirer

康奈尔大学教授


以太坊经典（ETC）自称攻击他们链的大可能是自私挖矿攻击。


实际上，自私挖矿可以用来降低双重花费攻击的成本。这样做可以使攻击者花费的哈希算力变少，但是代价是攻击成功的概率也降低。


鉴于一些我从别人那里听到的消息，我怀疑这次攻击不是自私挖矿，但也只有仔细调研才能得出最准确的结论。


Ethereum Classic said that the attacks against their chain were most likely Selfish Mining attacks.


Indeed, selfish mining can be used to reduce the cost of a double-spend attack. The trade-off is that the attacker needs less hashpower, but sacrifices some success probability.


Given what I've heard from others, I suspect this was not SM, but only a careful post-mortem can tell.
