以太坊君士坦丁堡及彼得堡硬分叉

定于 2 月 25 日进行


以太坊硬分叉协调员 Afri Schoedon 在 Reddit 上透露，下次以太坊下次硬分叉将于 2019 年 2 月 25 日，在区块 7,280,000 的高度激活。在经过 2018 年 10 月以及上个月两次升级取消之后，此次排期为该硬分叉的第三次排期。不同的是，此次升级，将有两个硬分叉在同一区块高度同时进行。首先将启动君士坦丁堡升级，按计划好的更新内容进行升级，紧随其后的分叉称为彼得堡硬分叉，它将使此前发现问题的协议失效。


Ethereum Constantinople And Petersburg Hard Fork Scheduled For February 25


Ethereum hard fork co-ordinator Afri Schoedon revealed on Reddit that the next fork will activate on February 25, 2019, at block #7,280,000. This is the third time it has been scheduled, after cancelling it once on October 2018 and another time last month. However, this time, two forks will be taking place on the same block. The first is Constantinople, which will come with all its planned improvement. The next fork is called Petersburg, and it will disable the protocols in which issues were identified previously.

门罗币或将于3月9日在区块

高度 1788000 启动硬分叉


门罗币(XMR) Reddit 账户的一个帖子指出，该社区计划在区块高度达到 1788000 时启动硬分叉升级，预计该升级将于 3 月 9 日实施。最近一项研究显示，85%的 XMR 网络算力是由 ASIC 挖矿设备提供，而非GPU挖矿设备，而门罗币社区希望改变这一现象。据该帖子称，硬分叉相关的细节和日期还没有最终确定，但是社区成员应对即将到来的更新做好相关准备。


Possible Monero Fork Upcoming on March 9th at Block 1788000


A recent post on the Monero (XMR) Reddit page indicates that the community may be planning on a hard fork at Block 1788000, estimated to occur on March 9th. The move comes in response to recent analysis that showed that 85% of the XMR network hashrate is done with AISC mining rigs as opposed to GPU mining, something that the Monero community seeks to avoid. According to the post, the details and date have not been finalized, but nonetheless members of the community should be ready for the upcoming switch.

美国证交会专员：并非所有的 ICO 均为证券


在一次题为“保护公众，同时培养创新和企业家精神”的演讲中，美国证交会（SEC）专员 Hester Peirce 谈到，尽管她大谈了区块链和加密货币意识形态平台的“本质”，但去中心化仍将 SEC 要处理的问题复杂化了。据 Peirce 称，当前的证券法是在“所有证券发行商都有一个领导人，可以对该机构的相关物料信息发布权威资讯”的假设下运行的。Peirce 还表示，由于区块链项目是由“各方进行贡献”而非中心化架构，因此“这艘船可能没有掌舵人。”Peirce 在最后敦促监管机构仔细考虑是否所有的加密产品都需要受到监管，她建议制定一套惩罚制度来取代全面禁令。


SEC Commissioner says not all ICOs are Securities


In a recent speech titled “Protecting the Public While Fostering Innovation and Entrepreneurship”, SEC Commissioner Hester Peirce explained that while she shares in the “very essence” of the ideological platform of blockchain and cryptocurrency, the issue of decentralization complicates the matter for the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). According to Peirce, the current securities laws operate under the assumption that “every issuer has someone at the helm who can authoritatively disclose the relevant material information about the organization.” Peirce also said that because blockchain projects “run on diffuse contributions” instead of centralized structures, “there may not be anyone steering the ship.” Peirce concluded by urging regulators to carefully consider whether all crypto-related products require regulation and she suggested the development of a penalty system in place of a blanket ban.

BCH 雪崩交易预示该网络的最终速度将比以太坊快10倍


过去几周内，BCH 社区一直针对Avalanche（雪崩） 协议进行热烈讨论，该协议是一种预共识算法。目前，该协议已应用于Bchd的全节点，该协议的概念证明也在比特币现金主网上运行。据 BCH 的开发者称，由于Avalanche 与主要的 PoW 共识机制同步运行，它将使得网络更加强大。据 Bchd 开发者称，在实施了 Avalanche 协议之后，交易可在1.55秒内完成，将比以太坊快十倍。BTC ABC 倡导者 Amaury Sechet 认为，Avalanche 也可以保护区块链免受区块重组攻击。


BCH Avalanche Transactions Reveal Finality Speeds are Ten Times Faster Than Ethereum


Over the past weeks, the BCH community has been discussing Avalanche, a pre-consensus method. Currently, the protocol has been used in the full node implementation of Bchd, and its proof of concept is running on the Bitcoin Cash mainnet. According to BCH developers, Avalanche will make the network stronger as it runs parallel with the primary PoW consensus mechanism. Per Bchd developers, after the  Avalanche implementation, transactions can be now be finalized after 1.55 seconds, ten times faster than Ethereum. Bitcoin ABC advocate Amaury Sechet believes Avalanche can also protect the blockchain from reorganization attacks.
