Guideline 3.2 - Business



We found that your app is an in-house app, intended for employees or members of your organization. As such, it is not appropriate for the App Store.


For information on distributing proprietary, in-house apps, please refer to the  Apple Developer Enterprise Program .


被判定为企业内部使用APP
解决方案（增加注册功能，对注册账号开放所有功能菜单），注册入口由后台控制开启关闭

Guideline 5.2.1 - Legal - Intellectual Property

The seller and company names associated with your app do not reflect the name of a financial institution in the app or its metadata, as required by Guideline 5.2.1 of the  App Store Review Guidelines.



到这里就没有再回复了
原因：（出现了贷款银行）被判定为科技公司没有发布金融贷款的资质，应该由该金融机构账号下发布
解决方案：后台控制隐藏贷款或银行相关文字，以及功能
