TokenLoan合伙人吕锡维：史诗级大牛市真要来？数字资产借贷将颠覆时代

  • 柚子社区

以比特币为代表的加密数字货币从2018年5月22日的8382.97美元一路狂跌至2018年6月29日的5893.33美元，上行艰难的走势一度影响了很多人的情绪。近日，利好消息频出，比特币暴涨超42%，重回8300美元。一位持币用户表示，两个月没看盘，一直忙于工作，币价还是稳稳的。相比较于传统投资品，他还是看好数字货币的发展前景。

 

Encrypted digital currency represented by bitcoin slumped from 8382.97 dollars in May 22th of 2018 to June 29th of 2018; this tough situation affected many people's faith. Recently, good news came out, the growth rate is more than 42%, the price returned to 8300 dollars again. a user with cryptocurrencies expressed "he didn't focus on the market for two months, busy at work,but the price is still stable ". Compared with the traditional currencies, he is more optimistic about the future of digital currencies.

                                                    注：2018年7月25日13:29，比特币价格8383.11美元

 

2018年7月25日，结束了首尔meetup的TokenLoan合伙人吕锡维先生受邀出席了珠三角城市群meetup。他表示：“基于区块链的数字资产质押借贷网络强势来袭，短短几月的时间，已经引起了整个区块链金融科技领域的高度重视。截至目前，已经有相当一部分头部投资机构表示愿意了解基于区块链的数字资产借贷业务的运作机制，并有大批持币用户表示该类业务的价值有待深挖。”

 

In July 25th of 2018, TokenLoan partner Winston，finishing the Korean meetup, was invited to participate the meetup in Pearl River Delta. He expressed that digital asset pledge loan platform based the blockchain comes with overwhelming force. It has attracted the high attention from the whole blockchain financial science and technology in several months. So far, a significant number of leading investment institutions have expressed their willingness to understand the operation mechanism of the digital asset lending business based on the blockchain, and a large number of currency users have indicated that the value of such business needs to be further explored.

 

                                              注：TokenLoan合伙人吕锡维先生解读数字资产借贷市场大趋势

 

 吕锡维先生强调：“趋势不等人！数字资产质押借贷加速了区块链金融科技的快速落地。如今比特币重回8300美元恰恰说明，数字货币信仰者对加密数字货币的信心不减。据悉，有个别乐观的业界人士表示，比特币极有可能重回并突破20000美元。”至于该追涨还是观望，他给出的建议是：“该来的终究会来！在别人贪婪的时候恐惧，在别人恐惧的时候贪婪。”

 

Winston emphasized “Time waits for no man”Digital asset pledge loan business accelerated the implementation of blockchain financial technology. Now, this condition that bitcoin price returned to 8300 dollars just expressed that digital currencies' believers have no less faith in encrypted digital currencies. By report, some optimists say it is highly likely to return and exceed $20,000. As for the follow or wait, he advised that what should come will come, be fearful when others are greedy. Be greedy when others are fearful. 

                                                      注：价值投资信仰者对加密数字货币信心不减

 

比特币即将暴涨？史诗级大牛市真要来？或许，这是短线炒币玩家密切关注的问题，但是诸多数字货币价值投资信仰者表示，短线持币不是他们最关心的，他们关注的是加密数字货币所引领的区块链时代的必然到来。

 

Bitcoin is going to have a huge growth? The epic bull market will come? Maybe, this is the point that short-term holders pay attention. But most digital currencies value investors expressed that they don’t care about the short-term point, what they care is the era of blockchain leaded by cryptocurrencies is inevitable.
