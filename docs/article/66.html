itBit Bitcoin Market Report: October 2015

2019/10/31 Bitcoin

September was another rough month for the global stock markets as the ongoing correction produced big losses and high volatility. Bitcoin remained range-bound throughout September moving between roughly $225-$240. Bitcoin’s stable price movements made it a better investment than many major global indices.  

Inside the October itBit Bitcoin Market Report

  • September Market Recap – Breakdown of key events that moved the price of bitcoin last month.
  • October Outlook – Three things bitcoin traders should keep an eye on this month.
