Introducing itBit’s New Trading Fee Structure

2019/10/31 Bitcoin

Transparency is one of itBit’s core values. Taking the time to answer questions, hold Reddit AMAs and publicly announce results of our USMS auction bids allows us to keep our customers well-informed of our activities and forge a deeper relationship with the bitcoin community.

Our no-fee trading promotion recently ended and we introduced low flat fees for our bitcoin exchange and OTC trading services. In the interest of transparency, below is a full breakdown of our new fee structure.

itBit Bitcoin Exchange

Low Flat Fee

A flat 0.20% fee is charged on taker trades for all traders with a 30-day trading volume under 8,000 bitcoins.

Opportunity to Trade Commission-Free

We will continue to waive maker trading fees, giving all customers the opportunity to buy and sell bitcoin for free on our exchange.

LEARN THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN MAKER AND TAKER TRADES

Special Rates for High-Volume Traders

We offer special low fees to retail and institutional traders with a 30-day trading volume over 8,000 bitcoins. Contact our ICG team at ICG@itbit.com to learn more.
