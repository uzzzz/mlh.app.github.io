Higher Withdrawal Limits Now Available

2019/10/31 Bitcoin

We have received a number of requests from our traders asking for larger daily and monthly withdrawal limits. Currently, itBit customers have default $2,500 daily and $15,000 monthly withdrawal limits which can be automatically increased to $15,000 daily and $300,000 monthly when Two Factor Authenticator (2FA) is enabled. 

Starting today, all itBit customers with 2FA enabled on their accounts have the opportunity to activate higher withdrawal limits. Click here to apply now!
